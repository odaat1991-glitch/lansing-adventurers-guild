Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring anything to my first session?

+

Not at all! We provide everything you need to get started, including dice, pencils, and a pre-generated character if you're joining a beginner's course. Just bring your imagination!

Where are the games hosted?

+

We partner with local game stores, libraries, and community centers in the Greater Lansing area. The exact location for your specific course or campaign will be provided upon booking.

What if I have to miss a session of an ongoing campaign?

+

We understand that life happens. Please provide at least 24 hours notice. While session fees are non-refundable, our DMs are skilled at weaving your character's absence into the story and will catch you up on your return.
