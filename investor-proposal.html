Strategic Outlook (SWOT Analysis)

Strengths

  • First-mover advantage in a defined market
  • Lean, low-capital business model
  • Diversified, high-margin revenue streams

Weaknesses

  • Initial dependency on key personnel (DMs)
  • Requires establishing new local partnerships
  • Limited initial brand recognition

Opportunities

  • Expansion into corporate team-building sector
  • Development of proprietary digital products
  • Franchise model potential for other college towns

Threats

  • Competition from free, informal play groups
  • Venue availability and cost fluctuations
  • Changes in discretionary consumer spending

Phased Growth Strategy & Milestones

P1
